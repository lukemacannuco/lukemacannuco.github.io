About the writer, Luke Macannuco:

My studies

I am currently a junior journalism major at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am also pursuing a Certificate of Professional Writing and Technical Communication. My journalistic interests include investigative journalism, arts and entertainment journalism, and general watchdog reporting.

My work

In my time as a student journalist, I have written for the Goucher College Quindecim in the Arts and Entertainment and News sections and the Massachusetts Daily Collegian in the News, Arts and Living, and Opinion-Editorial sections. Outside of student media, I worked as a reporting intern for the Harvard Press, a small local newspaper based in Harvard, Massachusetts, where I covered local politics. I am also a tutoring intern at the University of Massachusetts Amherst Writing Center, where I tutor undergraduate students, graduate students, and faculty on their writing.
