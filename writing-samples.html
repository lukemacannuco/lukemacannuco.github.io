As Told To Exercise

For my Art of the Profile class, we were assigned to write an "as told to" story, the task being to use only the interview subject's words in our reconstruction of their story. For this, I interviewed my mother about a childhood experience visiting her cat-obsessed aunt's home in Florida.

Tutoring Philosophy

For my Tutoring Theory class (as part of my training as a Writing Center tutor), I wrote a final paper on my developing philosophy as a Writing Center tutor, using both theory read in class and experiences in my practice.

America's Distortion of the Vietnam War

For my American War in Vietnam history class, I wrote my first paper on the distortions of the war by the American government, using secondary and primary sources to establish my argument.
